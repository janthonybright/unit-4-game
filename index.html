Crystal Collector!

Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You will win your game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts the crystals will have a new value.
